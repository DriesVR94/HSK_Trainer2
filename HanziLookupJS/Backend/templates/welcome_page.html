About HSK

The HSK Exam (Hànyǔ Shuǐpíng Kǎoshì, 汉语水平考试) is the official Chinese language proficiency test used globally to assess non-native speakers' proficiency in Chinese. Originally comprising six different levels, the new HSK version 3.0 now consists of nine levels.
